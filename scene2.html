200,00 people die in India each year due to lack of access to safe drinking water


Annual per-capita water availability of less than 1700 cubic meter is considered as water stressed condition whereas annual per-capita water availability below 1000 cubic meters is considered as a water scarcity condition. Fresh water availability has dropped more than 70% since 1951.


Agriculture is the largest user of water in India and the trend hasn't changed much in the last few years. Water use by sector data shows the total water consumption for an activity in a year and is categorized as agriculture (which includes dairy and allied sectors), industries and domestic use (including drinking water).
